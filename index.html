Premise

A small Ontario town is haunted by a judgment that returns whenever buried lies begin to surface.

The Rule

If the marks appear, there is one way out: a true confession, made before judgment arrives.

Tone

Gothic crime mystery, supernatural punishment, rural dread, candlelight, cold rooms, mirrors, and tally marks.
